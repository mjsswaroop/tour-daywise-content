# Grand Australia & Scenic New Zealand – Day-wise Itinerary

## Day 1 – Depart from Kolkata
Meet the Tour Director at Kolkata Airport and fly to Melbourne. Arrival, hotel check-in, and overnight rest.
Meals: Meals on Board

## Day 2 – Great Ocean Road
Enjoy a full-day drive along the Great Ocean Road with spectacular coastal views and visit the Twelve Apostles. Return to Melbourne for dinner and overnight stay.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 3 – Melbourne City Tour
Guided tour including War Memorial, Yarra River, Arts Centre, and Melbourne Cricket Ground. Dinner and overnight stay.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

## Day 4 – Cairns & Great Barrier Reef
Fly to Cairns and enjoy a cruise to Green Island at the Great Barrier Reef. Snorkelling, swimming, and reef exploration. Overnight stay in Cairns.
Meals: Packed Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

## Day 5 – Kuranda Rainforest & Sydney
Visit Kuranda village and Skyrail Rainforest Cableway. Fly to Sydney, dinner and overnight stay.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 6 – Sydney Highlights
Visit Bondi Beach, Sydney Opera House, Sydney Tower, Sea Life Aquarium, and enjoy a Showboat Dinner Cruise on Sydney Harbour.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 7 – Blue Mountains
Explore Blue Mountains National Park, Scenic Railway, Skyway, Cableway, Three Sisters, and Featherdale Wildlife Park.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 8 – Fly to Christchurch & Lake Tekapo
Fly to New Zealand. Drive to Lake Tekapo and enjoy scenic alpine views.
Meals: Packed Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 9 – Mount Cook & Queenstown
Visit Church of the Good Shepherd, Mount Cook, Lake Pukaki, Arrowtown, and Skyline Gondola ride in Queenstown.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 10 – Milford Sound
Full-day excursion to Milford Sound with cruise through fjords and waterfalls.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

## Day 11 – Franz Josef / Fox Glacier
Travel through scenic landscapes to Franz Josef / Fox Glacier area.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 12 – TranzAlpine Train & Christchurch
Enjoy the TranzAlpine scenic train journey to Christchurch. Dinner and overnight stay.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 13 – Christchurch & Optional Kaikoura
Optional Kaikoura tour (weather permitting) and orientation tour of Christchurch.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 14 – Auckland City Tour
Fly to Auckland. Visit Sky Tower and city highlights.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 15 – Rotorua & Glowworm Caves
Visit Waitomo Glowworm Caves and Rotorua geothermal attractions including Te Puia.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 16 – Return to Kolkata
Transfer to Auckland Airport and fly back to Kolkata.
Meals: Breakfast, Meals on Board
