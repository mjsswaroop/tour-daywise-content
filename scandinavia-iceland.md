# Scandinavia with Iceland – Day-wise Itinerary

## Day 1 – Flight to Copenhagen
The journey begins in Kolkata with assistance from the Tour Director. Fly via Doha to Copenhagen. On arrival, check in to the hotel, enjoy dinner, and relax for the night in Denmark’s charming capital.
Meals: Meals on Board, Dinner

## Day 2 – Copenhagen City Tour & DFDS Cruise
Explore Copenhagen including the Little Mermaid, Nyhavn Harbour, Gefion Fountain, and Amalienborg Palace (exterior). Walk along Strøget, Europe’s longest pedestrian street. Later, board the DFDS Cruise for an overnight journey with dinner onboard.
Meals: Breakfast, Light Lunch, Dinner on Board

## Day 3 – Oslo & Scenic Fjord Drive
Arrive in Oslo and enjoy a guided city tour including Vigeland Sculpture Park. Continue on a scenic drive toward the fjord region, reaching Gol or Geilo. Enjoy dinner and overnight stay near the Hardangerfjord gateway.
Meals: Breakfast, Light Lunch, Dinner

## Day 4 – Flåm Railway & Sognefjord
Board the world-famous Flåm Railway, enjoying panoramic mountain and waterfall views. Cruise through Sognefjord and the narrow Nærøyfjord. End the day with dinner and overnight stay in the Bergen area.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 5 – Bergen to Iceland
Explore Bergen city and enjoy a Fløibanen funicular ride for panoramic views. Fly to Iceland and continue to the Fludir region. Visit the Aurora Centre and check in for the night.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 6 – South Coast & Jokulsarlon
Travel along Iceland’s south coast visiting Skógafoss waterfall, Reynisfjara Black Sand Beach, Vik village, and the Jokulsarlon Glacial Lagoon. Return late to the hotel for dinner and rest.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 7 – Golden Circle Tour
Visit Thingvellir National Park, Geysir Strokkur, and Gullfoss Waterfall. Arrive in Reykjavik, visit Hallgrimskirkja Church, and enjoy free time before dinner and overnight stay.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 8 – Stockholm City Tour
Fly to Stockholm. Enjoy a guided city tour including Gamla Stan, Royal Theatre, Nobel Museum, and Stockholm Cathedral. Dinner at an Indian restaurant.
Meals: Packed Breakfast, Light Lunch, Dinner

## Day 9 – Stockholm to Doha
Transfer to Stockholm Airport and fly to Doha.
Meals: Breakfast, Meals on Board

## Day 10 – Return to Kolkata
Arrive early morning in Kolkata with unforgettable Scandinavian and Icelandic memories.
Meals: Meals on Board
