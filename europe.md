# Best of Europe – Day-wise Itinerary

## Day 1 – Kolkata to London
Meet the Tour Director at Kolkata International Airport and complete immigration and boarding formalities. Board the flight to London and begin your European journey.
Meals: Meals on Board

## Day 2 – London City Tour & Attractions
Arrive in London and visit Madame Tussauds Museum. Check in to the hotel and enjoy an early dinner. Optional visit to the London Eye and Thames River Cruise.
Meals: Meals on Board, Dinner

## Day 3 – London to Paris by Eurostar
Explore London’s landmarks including Big Ben, Trafalgar Square, Tower Bridge, Buckingham Palace, Piccadilly Circus, and St. Paul’s Cathedral. Later board the Eurostar train to Paris. Dinner and overnight stay in Paris.
Meals: Breakfast, Light Lunch, Dinner

## Day 4 – Paris Highlights
Visit the Eiffel Tower (3rd level) and enjoy a guided city tour covering Concorde Square, Champs-Élysées, Arc de Triomphe, and Trocadero. Explore the Louvre Museum. Optional Seine River Cruise or Lido Show.
Meals: Breakfast, Light Lunch, Dinner

## Day 5 – Paris to Switzerland
Drive through scenic landscapes to Switzerland and check in at Zurich. Enjoy dinner at an Indian restaurant.
Meals: Breakfast, Light Lunch, Dinner

## Day 6 – Mount Titlis & Rhine Falls
Ascend Mount Titlis by the Rotair cable car and experience snow-covered peaks. Visit the majestic Rhine Falls before returning to Zurich for dinner.
Meals: Breakfast, Light Lunch, Dinner

## Day 7 – Jungfraujoch (Optional) & Lucerne
Optional excursion to Jungfraujoch – Top of Europe. Visit the Sphinx observation deck and Interlaken. Later explore Lucerne including Chapel Bridge.
Meals: Breakfast, Light Lunch, Dinner

## Day 8 – Munich, Wattens & Innsbruck
Orientation tour of Munich followed by a visit to the Swarovski Crystal Museum in Wattens. Continue to Innsbruck or Seefeld for overnight stay.
Meals: Breakfast

## Day 9 – Innsbruck to Venice
Walking tour of Innsbruck including Maria Theresien Strasse and Imperial Palace. Travel to Venice and arrive by Vaporetto at St. Mark’s Square. Enjoy a gondola ride.
Meals: Breakfast, Light Lunch, Dinner

## Day 10 – Venice, Pisa & Rome
Visit the Leaning Tower of Pisa at the Square of Miracles. Continue to Rome and check in at the hotel.
Meals: Breakfast, Light Lunch, Dinner

## Day 11 – Rome & Vatican City
Visit Trevi Fountain, view the Colosseum (outside), drive past ancient Roman monuments, and explore Vatican City including St. Peter’s Basilica. Transfer to the airport for return flight.
Meals: Breakfast, Light Lunch, Meals on Board

## Day 12 – Return to Kolkata
Arrive in Kolkata with unforgettable memories of Europe.
Meals: Meals on Board
