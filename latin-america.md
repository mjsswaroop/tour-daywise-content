# Radiance of Latin America – Day-wise Itinerary

## Day 1 – Delhi to Rio de Janeiro
Depart from Delhi via Amsterdam to Rio. Arrival, hotel transfer, dinner, and overnight stay.
Meals: Meals on Board

## Day 2 – Rio de Janeiro Highlights
Visit Christ the Redeemer, Sugarloaf Mountain, Copacabana Beach, and Maracanã Stadium (outside).
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 3 – Rio to Manaus
City tour of Rio and evening flight to Manaus.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner on Board

## Day 4 – Amazon Rainforest
Boat transfer to rainforest lodge. Jungle activities and overnight stay.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

## Day 5 – Amazon Wildlife Experience
Canopy walk, boat tours, and wildlife exploration in the Amazon.
Meals: Breakfast, Packed Lunch, Dinner

## Day 6 – Manaus to Iguazu
Return to Manaus and overnight transfer to Iguazu.
Meals: Breakfast, Packed Lunch, Dinner

## Day 7 – Iguazu Falls (Brazil & Argentina)
Explore Iguazu Falls from both Brazilian and Argentine sides.
Meals: Breakfast, Packed Lunch, Dinner

## Day 8 – Iguazu Adventure
Great Adventure boat tour and panoramic viewpoints.
Meals: Breakfast, Packed Lunch, Dinner

## Day 9 – Buenos Aires
Fly to Buenos Aires. City tour and Tango show.
Meals: Breakfast, Packed Lunch, Dinner

## Day 10 – Ushuaia
Fly to Ushuaia, the southernmost city in the world.
Meals: Breakfast, Packed Lunch, Dinner

## Day 11 – Ushuaia National Park
Tierra del Fuego National Park, End of the World Train, and Beagle Channel cruise (optional).
Meals: Breakfast, Packed Lunch, Dinner

## Day 12 – El Calafate
Fly to El Calafate. Visit Historical Interpretation Centre.
Meals: Breakfast, Packed Lunch, Dinner

## Day 13 – Perito Moreno Glacier
Full-day visit to Perito Moreno Glacier with optional boat ride.
Meals: Breakfast, Meals on Board

## Day 14 – Lima
Fly to Lima. City highlights and overnight stay.
Meals: Meals on Board

## Day 15 – Cusco
City tour of Lima and flight to Cusco. Acclimatisation and rest.
Meals: Meals on Board

## Day 16 – Rainbow Mountain
Early morning trek to Rainbow Mountain.
Meals: Packed Breakfast, Packed Lunch, Dinner

## Day 17 – Machu Picchu
Scenic train journey and guided tour of Machu Picchu.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

## Day 18 – Cusco to Amsterdam
Fly from Cusco to Lima, then onward to Amsterdam.
Meals: Meals on Board

## Day 19 – Amsterdam
Arrive in Amsterdam.
Meals: Meals on Board

## Day 20 – Return to Delhi
Fly from Amsterdam to Delhi.
Meals: Meals on Board
