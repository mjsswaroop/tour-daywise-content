# African Safari – Day-wise Itinerary

## Day 1 – Kolkata to Nairobi
Meet the Tour Director at Kolkata Airport and fly to Nairobi. Dinner at the famous Carnivore Restaurant and overnight stay.
Meals: Meals on Board

## Day 2 – Lake Naivasha & Maasai Mara
Drive to Lake Naivasha for a scenic boat ride among hippos and birds. Continue to Maasai Mara and visit a Maasai Village.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

## Day 3 – Maasai Mara Safari
Full-day game drives in Maasai Mara with opportunities to spot the Big Five. Cultural interaction with the Maasai community.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

## Day 4 – Maasai Mara Safari
Another full day of wildlife safaris across the savannas of Maasai Mara.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

## Day 5 – Great Rift Valley & Lake Elmenteita
Drive through the Great Rift Valley. Game drive at Lake Nakuru followed by a peaceful stay at Lake Elmenteita.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

## Day 6 – Amboseli National Park
Travel to Amboseli National Park with Mount Kilimanjaro as a dramatic backdrop. Evening safari and overnight stay.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

## Day 7 – Amboseli Safari
Full-day safari in Amboseli, famous for its elephant herds and scenic landscapes.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner

## Day 8 – Nairobi Return Journey
Drive back to Nairobi with lunch en route and board the evening flight to Dubai.
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner on Board

## Day 9 – Return to Kolkata
Continue onward to Kolkata, concluding the African adventure.
Meals: Meals on Board
