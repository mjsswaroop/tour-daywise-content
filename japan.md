# Japan in Bloom – Day-wise Itinerary

## Day 1 – Kolkata to Tokyo
Meet the Tour Director at Kolkata Airport and fly to Tokyo via Kuala Lumpur. Arrival, dinner, and overnight stay.
Meals: Meals on Board, Dinner

## Day 2 – Tokyo City Tour
Visit the Imperial Palace (outside view), Shinjuku Gyoen Park, and Tokyo Skytree.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 3 – Mount Fuji & Shizuoka
Cruise on Lake Ashi, ascend Komagatake Ropeway, and visit Mount Fuji’s 5th Station or Visitor Centre. Continue to Shizuoka.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 4 – Tea Farm, Suzuki Museum & Mie
Visit a traditional tea farm and Suzuki Museum. Enjoy a ferry ride to Mie Dolphin Bay and coastal drive to Aichi.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 5 – Mikimoto Pearl Island & Kyoto
Visit Rapeseed Blossom Festival and Mikimoto Pearl Island. Continue to Kyoto.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 6 – Kyoto Heritage Tour
Visit Arashiyama Bamboo Grove, Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion), participate in a Japanese tea ceremony, and explore Kyoto Imperial Palace.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 7 – Kyoto to Hiroshima & Miyajima
Board the Shinkansen bullet train to Hiroshima. Ferry to Miyajima Island and visit Itsukushima Shrine.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 8 – Hiroshima & Osaka
Visit Peace Memorial Park and Atomic Bomb Dome. Bullet train to Osaka and visit the Osaka Flyer building.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 9 – Nara & Osaka
Visit Tōdai-ji Temple and Nara Park. Drive past Osaka Castle and explore Shinsaibashi Street.
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner

## Day 10 – Return to Kolkata
Transfer to Osaka Airport and fly back to Kolkata.
Meals: Meals on Board
